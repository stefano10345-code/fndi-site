About FNDI

Two founders on a mission to give small businesses the same AI tools that big corporations use — without the big corporation price tag.

Our Story

Stefano Fernandini and Douglas O'Keefe met in middle school in Miami. They always knew they'd build something together. When they saw how many small businesses were losing revenue to missed calls, unresponsive leads, and outdated systems — while big corporations had entire teams and million-dollar budgets dedicated to AI — they didn't think twice.

"It's not about the idea. It's about execution. We saw the problem and started building the next day. Our mission: give small businesses in South Florida the same powerful AI tools that Fortune 500 companies use — without the Fortune 500 price tag."

FNDI stands for Future Native Digital Intelligence. The name says it all: the future of business is native to AI. Every voice agent, chatbot, website, and automation tool we build is designed around one thing — helping your business grow.
