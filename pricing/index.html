FREQUENTLY ASKED

Common Questions

Is there a long-term contract?

No. All our plans are month-to-month. You can cancel anytime. If you pay annually, you get a 20% discount.

How long does setup take?

Most implementations are live within 7-14 business days. Voice agents and chatbots can be live in as little as 48 hours. Websites and CRMs take a bit longer.

What if I need to change plans?

You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. We'll prorate the difference so you only pay for what you use.

What does the setup fee include?

The setup fee covers custom AI training, integration with your existing systems, thorough testing, and a launch session with our team.

Will it work with my current software?

Yes. We integrate with most popular booking systems, CRMs, and business tools. If there's something we don't support yet, we'll build it.
