Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a long-term contract?

No. All our plans are month-to-month. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime without penalties.

How long does setup take?

Most projects launch within 7-14 business days. Voice agents and chatbots can be live in as little as 48 hours. Websites and custom integrations take a bit longer.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes. Pay annually and save 20% on your monthly rate. You still have the flexibility to cancel anytime.

Will it integrate with my existing systems?

Yes. We integrate with most popular booking systems, CRMs, and business tools. If we don't support something, we'll build it.

What if I need to upgrade or downgrade?

You can change plans anytime. We'll prorate the difference so you only pay for what you use.

What if my needs don't fit a standard plan?

That's exactly what our Custom plan is for. Book a call and we'll build a solution that fits your business perfectly.
