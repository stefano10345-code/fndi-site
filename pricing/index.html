FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost?

It depends on what you need. After our discovery call, we'll put together a custom proposal with clear pricing — no surprises, no hidden fees. Most clients invest between $300–$1,500/month depending on scope.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. We operate month-to-month. You can adjust your services or cancel anytime with 30 days' notice.

How long does setup take?

Most projects launch within 7–14 business days. Voice agents and chatbots can be live in as little as 48 hours. Websites and custom integrations take a bit longer.

Will it integrate with my existing systems?

Yes. We integrate with most popular booking systems, CRMs, and business tools. If we don't support something yet, we'll build the integration for you.

What if I only need one or two services?

That's totally fine. You're not locked into a full package. Need just a chatbot and some automations? We'll price it accordingly. Need everything? We'll build that too.

What happens on the discovery call?

It's a quick 15–20 minute conversation. We'll learn about your business, identify the biggest opportunities, and explain how we can help. No pressure, no sales pitch — just a real conversation.
