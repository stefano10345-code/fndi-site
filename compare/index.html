But What About...

Common questions about AI voice agents

Can AI really handle complex calls?

Absolutely. Our AI is trained to handle objection handling, appointment scheduling, emergency transfers, and detailed information collection. It learns your specific workflows and can handle nuanced conversations with the same professionalism as a seasoned receptionist.

What if customers prefer talking to a human?

Our AI gracefully transfers to a human agent at any point during the call. Customers don't feel like they're talking to a robot—they feel like they're getting premium service. In fact, 94% of customers are satisfied with AI handling, and they appreciate the instant response.

Is it hard to set up?

No. We handle the entire setup process for you in 5-7 days. Our team integrates with your systems, trains the AI on your specific workflows, and gets everything running smoothly. You'll have a dedicated success manager to ensure everything works perfectly.

What happens if the AI makes a mistake?

Human oversight is built in. You get detailed call transcripts, can review interactions, and our AI learns from every call. Plus, you set parameters for what gets escalated. It's more reliable than any single person because it never has a bad day.
