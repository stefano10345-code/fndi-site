How It Works

From discovery to launch in 4 simple steps. You'll have your AI system live and handling calls within 48 hours.

The FNDI Process

1

Discovery Call

Day 1 — 30 minutes

We analyze your business, understand your challenges, and design the perfect AI system for you.

What to expect:
  • Overview of your business & goals
  • Analysis of your call patterns
  • Demo of relevant AI tools
  • Custom quote & timeline
2

Custom Build

Day 1-2 — 6-12 hours

Our team builds your AI agent, customizes it for your business, and integrates it with your systems.

What we do:
  • Train AI on your FAQ & processes
  • Connect to your calendar & CRM
  • Set up phone forwarding
  • Create custom prompts & flows
3

Launch & Train

Day 2 — 2-4 hours

We launch your AI agent and train your team on how to use it and optimize performance.

What happens:
  • Go-live with live testing
  • Team training session
  • Real-time monitoring
  • Quick adjustments as needed
4

Optimize & Scale

Ongoing

We monitor performance, gather feedback, and continuously improve your AI system.

Monthly activities:
  • Performance analytics review
  • Call quality monitoring
  • AI prompt optimization
  • New feature rollouts
