Common Questions

How long does setup actually take? ▼
From discovery call to live launch: 2 weeks on average. The discovery call happens same-day or next day. We deliver your custom strategy within 48 hours. Build and launch takes 5-7 business days. The whole process is fast because we focus on what matters most to your business.
Do I need technical skills to use this? ▼
Not at all. Our AI systems are designed to be simple to use and manage. We handle all the technical setup and configuration. You get a simple dashboard to monitor performance and adjust settings. If you need changes, we handle them for you.
Can I cancel anytime if it doesn't work for me? ▼
Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime. Our goal is to deliver real value from day one, so if you're not seeing results, we'll work with you to optimize. Most of our clients stick around because the AI actually works.
How do you measure ROI? ▼
We track metrics that matter: calls handled, leads generated, time saved, customer satisfaction, and revenue impact. You get monthly reports showing these numbers. Most clients see ROI within the first 30-60 days.
