Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use our website or engage with our services. This includes:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, and business name — collected when you fill out a form, book a call, or email us.
  • Business information: Details about your business, industry, and needs — shared during consultations or onboarding.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referring URLs, device type, and browser — collected automatically through Google Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request.
  • Send you project updates, invoices, and service-related communications.
  • Improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • Analyze site traffic and usage patterns through Google Analytics.

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing unless you opt in. We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Form Submissions

When you submit a form on our website (such as the Free Assessment or Contact form), your information is sent directly to us via FormSubmit.co, a third-party form handling service. FormSubmit processes the data to deliver it to our email and does not store your submissions long-term. You can review FormSubmit's practices at formsubmit.co.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects data such as your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and time on site. This data is aggregated and anonymized — we cannot identify individual users from it.

Google Analytics uses cookies to track usage. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies, primarily for Google Analytics. We do not use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or tracking you across other websites. Your browser settings allow you to block or delete cookies at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

Our AI solutions may integrate with third-party platforms on your behalf (such as scheduling tools, CRMs, or phone systems). These integrations are set up with your knowledge and consent. Each third-party service has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them.

We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to deliver the services you've requested.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This includes using HTTPS encryption on our website and limiting access to client data to authorized team members only.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. If you request that we delete your data, we will do so within 30 days, unless we are legally required to retain it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of any marketing communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at stefano@fndi.tech.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are designed for businesses, not individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date below. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
