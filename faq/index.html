Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with FNDI.

GENERAL

What is FNDI?

SERVICES & PRODUCTS

What does an AI voice agent actually do?

+
An AI voice agent answers your business phone calls 24/7 with a natural, human-sounding voice. It can greet callers, answer common questions about your services, book appointments, take messages, and route urgent calls — all without any human intervention. Think of it as a receptionist that never sleeps.

What's the difference between a chatbot and a voice agent?

+
A voice agent handles phone calls — it speaks and listens in real time over the phone. A chatbot lives on your website and handles text-based conversations with visitors. Both can answer questions, book appointments, and capture leads, just through different channels. Many of our clients use both.

Can you build me a website too?

+
Absolutely. We build modern, fast, mobile-friendly websites that are designed to convert visitors into customers. Every site we build comes with AI tools integrated — chatbots, lead capture forms, analytics, and more. We handle everything from design to deployment.

What kind of automations can you set up?

+
Pretty much anything repetitive. Appointment reminders, follow-up emails, invoice generation, lead routing, review requests, social media posting, data entry — if you're doing it manually and it follows a pattern, we can automate it. We'll map out your workflow and build automations around how your business actually operates.

TECHNICAL

+
Not at all. We handle everything — setup, integration, training, and deployment. You just tell us what your business needs and we build it. If you can use a phone and check your email, you can use our tools.
